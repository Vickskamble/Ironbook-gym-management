Features

Everything You Need to Run Your Gym

From member onboarding to payment tracking, IronBook covers every aspect of gym management with a premium, modern interface.

🔐

Authentication & Roles

Secure login with role-based access — Superadmin, Admin, Trainer, and Staff. Each role sees only what they need.

👥

Member Management

Add, search, filter, and manage members with status tracking (Active, Expired, Paused). View complete member profiles.

📋

Plan Management

Create membership plans with custom pricing and durations. Plans auto-calculate expiry when recording payments.

🧾

Payment Tracking

Record payments linked to members and plans. Supports Cash, UPI, Card, and Bank Transfer. Auto-calculates expiry dates.

🏢

Multi-Gym Support

Run multiple gyms from one platform. Complete data isolation between gyms — each gym sees only its own data.

📊

Admin Dashboard

Superadmin panel with system-wide stats, staff management, and gym oversight. Manage everything from one place.
